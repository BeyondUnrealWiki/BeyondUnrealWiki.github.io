Category Modeling

Anything and everything in this category deals with the Modeling aspect of Unreal development. This includes tutorials and articles covering all 3 types of modeling: Player modeling, weapon modeling, and static mesh modeling. (Note we're using the US spelling of 'modeling', with one 'l'.)
