Who am I? What do I do? Why am I here?

Well ... I am BM.Deathwind (aka MP3Chuck, elsewhere). I am a student, [musician], and gamer. I'm also co-leader of what's becoming a TC for UT2k3, called Blades of Legends. I'll put a link when the site is no longer in "beta" stage. Hopefully we'll be hosted by PlanetUnreal by then... Basically, Blades of Legends will be a multi-player TC influenced by (but NOT based upon) the worlds of Warcraft and Lord of the Rings. Gameplay, at this point, will include Humans, Elves, and Orcs. Beyond that, things are still being changed/designed/confirmed. My job(s) are Project Lead, Music/Soundtrack, and some Coding. Possibly some collaborative mapping, as well. So hopefully I'll have plenty to contribute along the line. It will undoubtably be a long-term project...
