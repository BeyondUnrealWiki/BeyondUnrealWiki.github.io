Current projects:

SideScroller Mod

This semi-total conversion introduces a (kind of bizzare) mix of gameplay action into UT2004. The game comes with a few different modes of play - standard deathmatch, team deathmatch, capture the flag,and domination - as well as a totaly new single player gametype that is oriented towards achieving high scores.

The mod blends 3rd person and side scrolling views, as well as introducing a cell-shaded look, and gameplay that is reminiscent of Jazz-Jackrabbit 2 to create a whole new experience in UT2004.

ModMelee Duel

An add-on pack of melee weapons for Chaos UT2. The mod introduces special moves for each weapon.

UT2004 weapon and mutator mods

A bunch of small projects for UT2004

UT Classic weapon and muator mods

Some new weapons and mutators for the classic UT.
