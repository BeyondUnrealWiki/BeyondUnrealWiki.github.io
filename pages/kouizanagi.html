Who is Kou Izanagi?

It's not like it really matters, but the name Kou Izanagi (Kou for short) is really just a bunch of random stuff I just threw together when I was trying to come up with a creative name for Deus Ex. Gradually, the name has become a symbol for me for some reason. I've been working with Unreal Script (mainly Deus Ex's variant) for about a year, but I hadn't been serious with it until around three months ago. I am currently working on a modification right now, but I don't really have much to go on, so that's why I am here at UnrealWiki. I'm hoping that the more seasoned scripters could lend me a hand with the bugs, problems, and the replication. Also, art tends to be a strong suit of mine, but I cannot model a organic being for anything, so I am going to need to contact someone to do several of the things in the mod. Hmm... maybe I should point out that I'm an undisciplined mapper who's afraid of public opinion? Nahhhh....

Why should I care?

I don't know. Why are you reading this? If you don't have a reason, you must be bored, so there is no reason for you to care.
