SniperRifle (UT)

UT : Actor (UT) >> Inventory (UT) >> Weapon (UT) >> TournamentWeapon >> SniperRifle (Package: Botpack)

The SniperRifle is, well, a rifle meant for long-range sniping. The secondary fire of this weapon zooms in the player's view. Some players also choose to use this weapon as a high-powered short-to-mid range weapon without the using the zoom feature. The SniperRifle has a much higher rate of fire than similar weapons in other games, making this shorter-range use of the weapon quite deadly.

See SniperRifle for UT2003's sniping weapon, the Lightning Gun.
