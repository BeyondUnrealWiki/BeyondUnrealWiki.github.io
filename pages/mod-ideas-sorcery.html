Mod Ideas/Sorcery

Mod Ideas for UT2003 – Sorcery

Description

From my obsession with fantasy books and roleplaying comes the desire to ditch all the weapons and replace them with spells. This could probably be done with weapons alongside magic, but I'd like to leave that to one side for now. Adrenaline probably isn't much use in this unless some new combos can be implemented, because all the adrenaline stuff is really magic anyway.

The Spells

Basically implemented as new weapons, probably with a common ancestor class derived from a suitable Weapon base class (at risk of reimplementing projectile launching code). All spells would draw from the same mana pool, although differing amounts for each one of course. Mana pool implementation could be as simple as a new ammo type, connected to part of the mutator which replenishes it over time, and probably mana boost pickups as well. So far I'd definitely like to implement at least the following spells:

Fireball
a nice classic one, which will hopefully end up looking so good people stand still to watch it coming for them
Lightning bolt
probably not the same effect as the lightning gun, although no doubt it'll start out that way
Chaser
attaches itself to a target and hunts them down (within limits), then explodes
Heal
takes away some mana, gives you back some health
Shield
short-term protection from offensive spells
Lift
floats you upwards until your mana runs out. Primary fire - lift up. Secondary fire - slow your descent
Drain
steals target's mana, gives you health
Invisibility
like the adrenaline combo
Teleport
reimplementation of the translocator as a spell, probably with a different targeting system (eventually at least)

Eventually, however, some more offensive spells will probably prove necessary to keep some variety going. Might be worth thinking about things which are less like conventional weapons - point and click 'make the ground here ignite' type spells perhaps - with suitable adjustment to make sure they're not too unfair on the people nearby that spot of course. It would also be fun to see about telekinetic manipulation of other players - push, pull, maybe even lifting or throwing. Could be fun in CTF-Face3 anyway Obviously players would need a way to avoid or counter such spells being used to just throw them randomly off the edge.
