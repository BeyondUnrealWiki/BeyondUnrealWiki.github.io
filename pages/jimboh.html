About Me

My name is James. I got started in the UT mod scene since mid 2003.

Back then I was working on a RTS total conversion project called Global Domination for UT 2003. Unfortunately, due to lack of time (and later, interest) this project was scrapped in early '04. If anyone is interested on how I did my mouse/overhead code, I'd be more than glad to share it, but most of my code is largely uncommented, so I probably won't be making a tutorial on this. After a hiatus of about a year (where I had to deal with college apps, and RL™) I've regained interest in modding and I'm currently working on implementing my SyMMO Framework for UT2004.
