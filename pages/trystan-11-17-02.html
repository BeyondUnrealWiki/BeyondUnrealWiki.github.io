11/17/02 Difference in methodology.

I thought it funny that one of the guys on my team mentioned today that "we have different ways of doing things." I prefer to research and learn before typing a single line of code, ala the explanation of Object.uc above. He prefers to just jump in and change things to see what happens. The two methods seem to work well together as I'm usually able to spot what he's done wrong but he's able to produce code much faster than I am.
