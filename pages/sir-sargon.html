Sir Sargon

Ok so I should've made a page a while ago I bet. I've been using this place for information since... uh, last year I think. I found it while in my quest for knowledge to complete my rework of the Holy Wars mod. That mod, by the way, was completed rather nicely and is available. The next version of it is almost out as well, it promises to be nice. For a taste of V3 go to my UT page, listed below.

Oh and maybe you'd like to know something about me too. Well I'm an engineer, recently out of RPI with a shiny new degree in Mechanical Engineering. I've had a lot of Unreal Tournament experience, lots of UT mapping and coding experience too. My game skills aren't that hot because I just have fun and concentrate on thinking "Hey, how can I make this game better for everyone..." My skills at mapping and coding though are quite good. I have a programming background and my apparent engineering background to help me out. My list of accomplished projects though is rather short partly for lack of good ideas that motivate me and partly because I won't release something to the public unless I trust it won't fail.

Recently I learned a valuable lesson, watch for mispellings in your serverpackages list when debugging. I couldn't see why instagibv3 wasn't working in online tests, it worked but the weapon was invisible. Well, I had mispelled the package name on all (3 or 4) servers I tested on and that was the problem! I'm not sure why serverpackages is a big deal when all pcs have the mutator files already but hey, it is.

It's juts a polishing version really, but well worth the extra 50kb added to the file size. You may notice, if you look at teh changes to teh testing lab page, My development schedules are rather short. This update, V4, was written in a matter of a few days.
