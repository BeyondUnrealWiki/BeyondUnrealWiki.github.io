Weapon Availability

Not fair! I only have assault rifle/enforcer!

This section is short and sweet – no one likes not having a decent weapon to run around with.

Sure – some maps are designed so the player must use his/her brain to find a good weapon but don't go over the top -? make sure at least a weaker weapon is available at the start of a round, then they can traverse the map after that to gain a better one... and always make sure released players get access to some sort of weapon, preferably an decent one so they can defend themselves from jail-door campers. This is not a guideline it is a command goddammit!

Release protection is good, and helps, but it doesn't last forever, so it's not enough on its own.
