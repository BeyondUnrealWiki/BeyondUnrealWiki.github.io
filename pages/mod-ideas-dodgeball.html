Mod Ideas/Dodgeball

Mod ideas for UT 2003 – UT Dodgeball

Description

by Novacane

This is a rather simple idea. Each player joins the game with a dodgeball. That's the only weapon. You throw it, you have to go pick it up or get someone else's ball. This mod can be used on any type of game as it is just a different kind of weapon. The play mechanics would work like this. You have a ball. you can run around and just toss it at someone, and hitting them does some damage. Head shots do extra damage. If you hold the button down, you will stop moving and start to pull the ball back, charging up a power meter. The higher the meter is when you release the button, the harder you throw the ball and the more damage you do. There is no catching or deflecting of balls thrown at you (people could get too good at it and take away from the fun). This would be the basic version of the game. There would also be an option to put in powerups to take the place of normal pickups in the game. These powerups would give you the ability to perform different kinds of attacks, like a flaming dodgeball, exploding dodgeball, homing dodgeball, etc... the most common powerup would be the returning dodgeball, which would cause the ball to bounce back to you after hitting something.
