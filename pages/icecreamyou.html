About Me

I'm a casual mapper who loves the Unreal series. I like originality in maps, but I haven't produced anything particularly good yet as I still need to learn a 3D modeling program. I prefer UT2004, but am waiting anxiously for UT2007 and its editor. I am very active on the BUF mapping forum, and somewhat active on other sites.

Plans for my Website

My page with all the tutorials I'm going to write is up. You can check it out just to see what will be offered (Go to http://www.IceCreamYou.com and click Tutorials). Many of the tutorials I've written so far are incomplete or need reformatting but some of them are fine...

Current Projects

I'm working on a map temporarily called CTF-Decisions. It's a very unusual asymetrical outdoor map with much thought going into layout; you'll be pleasantly surprised with the z-axis play and the multiple routes you can take. It's currently about 85% complete but the remaining 15% might take awhile...

It's set 40 years into the future, when global warming has begun taking its toll. It's inside a giant greenhouse constructed by scientists to preserve some of the beauty of nature.

I'm also working on a map called AS-Borderline with |*BILLY$Clint*|. It's temporarily "on hold."
