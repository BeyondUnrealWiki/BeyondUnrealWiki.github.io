Delete Reply When You See It

A WikiTag.

It works like this:

Person A writes: what does foo do?

Person B answers: it does what it says above. Delete Reply When You See It.

Person A reads the reply, sees the tag, and removes both question and answer from the page.

No-one other than Person A should act on this Tag. However:

  • if it's been weeks... I mean weeks...
  • anybody who sees it might decide to change the tag to Refactor Me.
