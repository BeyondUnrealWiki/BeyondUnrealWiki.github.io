UEmitter Overview

What is UEmitter?

UEmitter is an advanced particlesystem for UT inspired by the emitter of UT2004 it is designed to give mappers advanced and flexible features for creating many different particle effects ranging from weather effects to explosions to plants and grass and whatever else your imagination can cook up
