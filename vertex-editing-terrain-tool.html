Vertex Editing Terrain Tool

This is one of the tools available in Terrain Mode.

  • Click the terrain to mark an area as selected
  • CLTRL + click adds to the existing selection in a weird fuzzy way which freezes UnreaEd for about 10 seconds hwile it thinks about it

once terrain is selected:

  • CTRL + both mouse buttons, then drag up or down to raise or lower
